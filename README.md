# Follow The Light
This project is a 3D physics-based game made with Unity &amp; C#

As a glowing light ball lost in the deepest of darkness, each time you collide with a wall or an object, you transfer some of your light to it, slowly revealing the path that you should follow.

Explore your way through the darkness while enjoying music and solving optical puzzles until you find a way out.

## Move the glowing ball around by appling force
Be aware of acceleration and try to balance the ball around, otherwise you'll fall from the platforms.

## Rotate the camera to change your perspective
Darkness can be treacherous, and some objects are only visible if some light is shed on them.

## Jump in the same spot to bounce higher each time
Use this to reach elevated platforms, or profit from this extra height to your advantage.

## Quickly jump back and forth between two walls
Use this to climb your way up when between very high parallel walls.

# Learning Outcomes
This project helped me learn about:
- Handling player & camera movemement and rotation in a 3D setting.
- Handling player controls and checking if the control's action is allowed to happen (e.g.: a player can't jump while mid air).
- Working with rigidbody, triggers, colliders, and physics raycasts (e.g.: raycasts would be used to know if a player is on the ground).
- Working with Unity's animator component and courotines (e.g.: transitioning from main menu screen to gameplay screen).
